ABOUT US

Parents in the UK, who seek to offer their kids wholesome Eleven Plus preparation, often get disillusioned with the choices online. If they get materials and test papers, they don’t get timed tests; if they get tests, they don’t get performance metrics; if they get metrics, they don’t get progress reports. And, above all, if they get a bunch of these, the service would be unaffordable.

Observing the plight for years, we decided to make all this and more available at pocket-friendly prices. We interacted with parents, children, and educators. Based their inputs, we made an online Eleven Plus preparation platform which caters to all their needs. Thus was born ElevenAce.

As ElevenAce is very user-friendly, kids can navigate it easily. We have scores of topics and thousands of questions covering Eleven Plus curriculum in-depth. Each topic has practice and assessments and kids also get to view the questions they got wrong, retry and learn it.

Our AI-driven platform gives parents regular progress reports mentioning kids’ strong and weak areas. Parents can easily view their kid’s progress in the website.

In addition, our mock tests give a real-time feel of the Eleven Plus and get kids ready to Ace it.

All this at just $120/yr per year.
