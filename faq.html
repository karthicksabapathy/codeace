Analytics

Percentage of completion shows the portion of total questions the kid has attempted in each subject. This shows how much more the kid has left to complete.
Leadership board shows the top performers among all ElevanAce child members in the same year. It is shown based on their score. The boards are displayed based on overall performance and subject-wise performance. Encourage your child to be on the leader board!
ElevenAce gives you four major performance metrics. Percentage of completion, your answers, reports, and leadership board.
  • Percentage of completion shows the portion of total questions the kid has attempted in each subject.
  • Your answers’ shows the percentage of answers the kid got right out of the total questions.
  • Reports give the average score of the kid in each section.
  • Leadership board lists the 10 top scorers among all Elevenace users along with your kid’s position.

In the reports section, you can sort the average score either in ascending or descending order. If you sort it in ascending order, the sections where your kid scored less would be at the top. These are the areas in which your kid is weak.

If you sort it in descending order, the sections where your kid scored well would be at the top. These are the areas in which your kid is strong.
