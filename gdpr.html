GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation

The protection of personal data and privacy in the European Union region is regulated by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679, a regulation in European Union (EU) law. The GDPR mainly aims to empower individuals over their personal information and how it is used by a service provider or a website. It was implemented on 25 May 2018.

To read the GDPR in detail, visit https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2018/12/contents/enacted

Invate Learning Solutions Ltd. collects personal data of parents and kids which are uploaded in this website. By definition Invate Learning Solutions Ltd. is the data controller.

We have taken the appropriate measures to ensure that our services comply with the GDPR. Such as hosting our information in cloud servers in the European Economic Area (EEA), encrypting our money transaction channels, and making our data transactions transparent.
